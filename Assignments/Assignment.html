My Dear PW classmates,

I would like to humbly request that we avoid copying and pasting or blindly typing answers to our assignments from any source. Instead, let us take the time to understand the concepts and apply them in our own words. This will not only help us learn better but also prevent any potential academic integrity issues.

Copying and pasting answers from the internet or other sources may seem like a quick and easy way to complete our assignments, but it does not benefit us in the long run. We need to develop our critical thinking and writing skills to succeed in our academic and professional lives.

Rewriting the information in our own words is also an excellent way to ensure that we fully understand the concepts we are learning. By putting the information into our own words, we can check our comprehension and identify any areas where we need to do further research.

Furthermore, copying and pasting from the internet or other sources without proper attribution is a form of plagiarism. This can result in severe consequences, including failing the assignment, failing the class, and even expulsion from the institution. Therefore, it is crucial that we always cite our sources and provide proper attribution for any information that we use in our assignments.

In conclusion, let us take pride in our work and avoid taking shortcuts. Let us put in the effort to understand the concepts and present our own thoughts and ideas in our assignments. This will not only benefit us but also help us become ethical and responsible scholars.

Kiran
